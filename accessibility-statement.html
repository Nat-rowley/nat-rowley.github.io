Accessibility Statement

Designed to be as accessible and usable for all humans. This accessibility statement sets out the compatibility and limitations of my web portfolio.

Standards and Guidelines

I strive to meet Level AA compliance for the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2. These are guidelines that explain how to make web content easier to use for disabled people. Additionally, I seek to go beyond compliance-based considerations alone and include Inclusive Design as I add to and maintain it.

Measures Taken

  • Testing with zoom
  • Testing on multiple platforms
  • Testing keyboard navigation
  • Testing with screen readers

Known Issues and Feedback?

Currently, there are no known accessibility issues. However, if you have any accessibility or usability feedback to help contribute to my portfolio, please feel free to contact me, thanks!

Statement Updates

This statement was prepared on 11 July 2024. It was last reviewd on 11 July 2024. Although I cannot guarantee that all content linked to, or embedded on this site is fully accessible, I try to choose sites and services that embrace accessibility compliance and inclusive design principles
